Institut Agrocampus & Seoul National University : A bottom-up approach to statistical analysis

An R training course for understanding perceptual data

Moonbin Jo

The objectives of this series of exercises are multiple. To help the student, the scientist, the researcher interested in perception data to make the most of them, to analyse them with R, to develop a “statistical” thinking. Making the most of perception data means knowing how to formulate research questions, knowing what lessons to draw from the data.

Data

  • Perception data - participant based perception data for environmental habits.
  • Perception data - constraint score based perception data for environmental habits, but modified for the scores of the habits to be the rows of the data, not the participants.
  • Perception data - constraint score based and modified long data that is modified specifically for Sessions 3, 4, and 5. For more information about how this modification was done, see Session 3.
